No. Our AI Fundamentals and Python for AI programs are designed for complete beginners and build up to advanced topics.

Yes. AAI credentials are project-verified and recognized by our hiring partners across the AI industry.

Can working professionals manage the schedule?+

Absolutely. Programs include self-paced modules plus optional live mentorship on evenings and weekends.

Will I get hands-on projects?+

Every program is project-first. You graduate with a portfolio of real, deployed AI systems.

We offer scholarships, EMI options and free introductory programs so learning AI stays accessible.
